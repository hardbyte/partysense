PartySense is the best way to make sure you have a great night out.

How it works

Party Animals!

  1. Event Invite

    Your DJ creates an event on partysen.se and puts the link on facebook

  2. Vote on the tracks

    The DJ will start by adding a selection of tracks to the event. Tell them what tracks you like by voting on them!

  3. Help choose the party music!

    Add music to the setlist - if the host has allowed it you can search for tracks from the extremely large Spotify music library.

    The DJ will see how popular each of the tracks are.
