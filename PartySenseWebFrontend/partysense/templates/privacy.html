Privacy Policy

Guiding Principles

Partysen.se respects your privacy and will not ever share personally identifiable information about you to any third party.

We aim to provide online collaborative music playlists. To achieve that we stand on the shoulders of giants. Specifically Facebook, Spotify and Lastfm.

What do we share, and with whom?

To create the best party environment possible, we show the host all of the tracks that have been added to an event, as well as all of the votes on those tracks.

We don't ever display who voted on a particular track. Nor do we ever display who added a particular track to an event.

In the future we plan to show non-identifying information about the most popular music to clubs. This will always be in the aggregate form - statistics created with information from all of our users.

Information from Facebook

To allow a seamless experience partysen.se can use a facebook login for authentication. We ask for standard permission as well as the ability to see your music. This music information is not cached or recorded. Your music data is requested from facebook purely to provide a list of music you may want to add to an event.

At this stage we have no plans to post information back to facebook, if that changes we will always explicitly ask the user first.

Information from Spotify

This product uses a SPOTIFY API but is not endorsed, certified or otherwise approved in any way by Spotify. Spotify is the registered trade mark of the Spotify Group. Spotify Metadata terms of use.

We use Spotify extensively to provide relevant search results, and to preview the setlist, you do not need a Spotify account to use this service.

If you import music from Spotify into an event, we treat it as if the music was added any other way (e.g. from the search or recent tracks).

Information from last.fm

We plan to implement importing of music, as well as music suggestions using the last.fm service.
